Hello everyone I'm Harsha Kumavat.I am a Data Engineer at Samhita. I hold a bachelor's degree in computer science and a master's degree in information technology.

What interests me the most is data. Data presented as graphs, charts, or dashboards is significantly simpler to comprehend compared to data saved in CSV or Excel files. Since I was a child, I've adored pictures because they make concepts easier to grasp than reading through extensive amounts of text. My fascination with data analysis stems from the ability to summarize vast volumes of data and extract valuable insights. However, as I transitioned into data engineering, I've become equally intrigued by the process of building and optimizing data pipelines to ensure seamless data flow for analysis.

The data visualization stage still intrigues me the most. A picture really is worth a thousand words, and I can use a variety of charts to visualize and present results in the form of interactive dashboards and reports. My goal is to assist businesses in making the most of their data so they can make better data-driven decisions.
