Hello everyone I'm Harsha Kumavat. I hold a bachelor's degree in computer science and a master's degree in information technology.

What interests me is data. In comparison to data saved in csv or excel files, data presented as graphs, charts, or dashboards is significantly simpler to comprehend. Since I was a little child, I have adored pictures since they make concepts easier to comprehend than while reading through voluminous amounts of text. I'm fascinated about data analysis since it allows for the summary of vast volumes of data in order to draw out valuable insights. The data visualization stage intrigues me the most. Because a picture really is worth a thousand words, I can use a variety of charts to visualize and present the results in the form of interactive dashboards and reports. My goal is to assist businesses in making the most of their data so they can make better data-driven decisions.
